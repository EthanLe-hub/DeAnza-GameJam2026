Synopsis:

You own a lovely flower shop that was passed down to you by your late grandparents. Wanting to share the love and keep their memories alive, you decide to follow their footsteps. The game is simple: help create flower bouquets for your customers! Over the course of five days, a unique customer comes in to request a bouquet with specific requirements. Based on your overall performance, you’ll receive different ending scenes on the last and final day, Valentine’s Day!

How to play:
1. Customers request a bouquet with simple requirements.
2. Click the arrow to be transferred to the flower bouquet workshop.
3. Each bouquet has five slots for each flower.
4. Click a flower for each slot.
5. Once done, click the arrow for submission.

Note:

Some customers are unique.
They have ongoing stories.
Their requests may be more general instead of clearly listing flowers.
Pay attention to what they say.
Unique customers may return depending on how well you do.
You can improve your relationship with them.
Your choices in flowers matter.
